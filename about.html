About the Club

Welcome to the Solar Flying Club! We’re a group of aviation enthusiasts who share a passion for flying. Solar Flying Club's mission is to stimulate and encourage interest in the study of aviation through a cooperative, safe and economical environment for all active members. We are a non-profit, equity ownership club who own, fly and maintain a Cessna 172M, an IFR-capable aircraft available for everything from a short local tour or lunch outing to extended trips located at Ankeny Regional Airport (KIKV) in Ankeny, Iowa.

Mission and Vision

At Solar Flying Club, we’re more than just a group of pilots – we’re a family. Our vision is to build a close-knit community around the plane we all proudly own, sharing our collective love for aviation. We believe that flying brings people together, and our vision is to create an environment where members can connect, learn, and grow through their shared passion for the skies.

Membership Benefits and Fees

As a member of Solar Flying Club, you'll enjoy access to our well-maintained Cessna 172M Skyhawk. Here are the membership details:

  • Initial deposit: $3,000
  • Monthly membership fee: $87
  • Hourly flight rate: $50 (tach time)
  • Refundable initial deposit upon successful sale of membership, minus a $50 administrative fee

Our History

We have a rich history that dates back to September 17, 1953, when three passionate pilots – Howard Heidman, Claude Gaskins, and John Beaman – came together to form a social flying club. With an initial membership fee of just $35, they started the club to promote camaraderie and fellowship among pilots in Central Iowa. We’ve grown a lot since then, but we’ve always stayed true to our roots.

About Ankeny Regional Airport (KIKV)

Ankeny Regional Airport opened in 1994 and serves as a reliever airport to Des Moines International Airport (KDSM). It’s a fantastic facility with a full-service FBO, Exec1 Aviation, Inc, offering aircraft maintenance, rental, car rental, primary flight training, and charter services. It’s the perfect home for our club.
