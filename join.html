Join Solar Flying Club

We welcome any and all interest to join Solar Flying Club! With our membership changing as people come and go, we recommend reaching out via our Contact Us page to first learn about our club and get your name on our waiting list by completing and submitting our application for membership. Club application is available for download.

What Makes a Great Member/Equity Owner of SFC:

  • Passionate about aviation, wanting to further that passion in the community, and eager to learn and grow in a group setting.
  • Interested in contributing as an airplane owner, which means helping out in club wash and waxes.
  • Currently a pilot! To fly in our club, you are required to have at least a recreational pilot certificate (Private Pilot or greater preferred) and current medical certificate (Basic Med accepted).

Our Club Rates

  • $3,000 share value refundable once membership is sold*
  • Monthly dues: $87.00
  • Flying rate: $50.00/tach hour dry

*Less administration fee currently set at $50

FAQ

To join, please contact us via our Contact Us page to express your interest. You can download the membership application here and submit it as instructed.

Only fees associated with the club are the initial, monthly, and hourly fees, no hidden fees here!

Reach out to us via our contact us page, fill out an application, meet with the new member committee, and lastly get checked out in N9156H by one of our CFI's! All this can be done in a week or so, after completion of checkout you will recieve access to the schedule and will offically be a part of the club!

We organize regular social gatherings like barbecues, club-wide plane detailing, and yearly meetings. These events are a great way to connect with fellow aviation enthusiasts and enhance your flying skills.

Members are expected to treat the plane like their own because you do own it! We also expect you do things like wipe the plane down and fuel the plane after your flight. Your involvement helps maintain the club's strong community and ensures the aircraft remains in top condition.

As a member of the Solar Flying Club, you are covered by the club's full insurance for aircraft damage and liability, as long as there is no gross negligence or violation of Federal Aviation Regulations. You do not need to buy additional insurance. For more details, feel free to contact us!

We hold an annual organizational meeting within 90 days of the end of the fiscal year. Additionally, general and special meetings can be called as needed for information sharing, social events, and safety meetings.

Scheduling flight time is easy! Members use our online scheduling system at www.aircraftclubs.com. Each member can schedule up to four time periods of one day or longer, with a maximum of two on weekends (Friday, Saturday, and Sunday). If you need to cancel a scheduled flight, please do so as soon as possible to allow other members to book the aircraft.

Our Maintenance Officer oversees all aspects of aircraft maintenance to ensure they are in top condition. Regular inspections and necessary repairs are conducted as per FAA regulations. All Maintenance and variable costs are covered by hourly rate and not at the expense of the member.
